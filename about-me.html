About Me

My life as a martial artist is neither interesting nor worthy of explaining here. However, for the sake of solidarity, I will explain a few things about myself.
I have a deep love for physicality and training. I believe that movement is capable of being a panacea for a multitude of severe chronic diseases and maintaining or improving mental acuity.
As such, martial arts and subsequently this website and other projects are to be observed as passions of mine. Passion projects that span a lifetime.
Within the 15 years or so I've been training, I've had the pleasure to train with world class martial artists and instructors, of which I will list below for your reference. I will also list the styles that have been the most influential on me over the years.
Outside of training, I love my family and choose to spend most of my free time with my wife and son. There is nothing that I need to see or know beyond them.
